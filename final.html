Final Project

Java Quest is a text-based rpg. Choose your path carefully! Each choice is a roll of the dice. The numbers that you roll will determine your fate. If your HP reaches zero, then you have been killed. Just hope that you don't roll the dreaded "13"! Restart by clicking on the "Main" button. I wanted this game to feel like an old rpg from the 90's. I had a lot more ideas of stuff to add to it but ran out of time. If you check back here, I will likely keep updating it! Thanks for playing!
